Project Information

Project Goals:

Create a way for ride share drivers to interact with one another similar to Facebook

Allow drivers to see what events are going on in the area

Provide a way for drivers to record their expenses, such as gas and maintenance

See our presentations below:

During this project, we followed the scrum method of software development and had 3 sprints where we worked on different elements of our application.

You will see that our project developed more as we went from one sprint to the next.

Mock-Ups:

Login/Sign Up

On this page, users are able to create an account and then log into that account.

User Screens

On the home screen, this is where drivers are able to post statuses similar to applications like Facebook

On the events page, this is where drivers would be able to see which events are happening in the areas so they know where to go to pick up the maximum number of riders

On the hotspots page, this is where drivers would be able to see where popular areas are

On the business page, this is where drivers can log their expenses and track how much they have spent on expenses

Messaging

This feature would allow drivers to communicate with one another and tell them what areas to avoid and what areas to go to.

Drivers could also welcome new drivers to the area and give advice.
